Corebooting a Thinkpad X200

Latest Update: 24-10-2022


CAUTION: When flashing coreboot, there's a risk of bricking your computer.

For this guide, you will need external programming equipment. (We recommend a Bus Pirate or a Raspberry Pi)


WARNING: X200s with LED Screens must have their cable and inverter with a CCFL-compatible version to work with AFFS screens.

X200 Flashing

There are two different flashchips used on the X200: An 8 pin 4MiB, and a 16 pin 8MiB, located just under the palmrest.
Make sure to find out your type before deciding to buy a 8-pin or 16-pin SOIC clip.
